The Wrench Bench

Combining privacy with efficiency

Based on the dimensions of a regular office chair combined with the dynamics of a train station bench, I designed a public seating area with Vectorworks and Artlantis that would balance public and private spaces with the comfort of home furniture. By making the bench circular while dividing areas with armrests, I was able to make the most of my seating area while giving visitors their own corner of the bench.
