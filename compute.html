Pyka Computer

Redesigning an ad for the Modern Age

For this project, I chose Lucian Bernhard’s poster for Adler typewriters from 1908 as I was amazed by its balance of saturated colors and object space that made it as popular, defined his Sachplakat style. The beautiful shades of purples of the typewriter and the font of the Adler logo on the orange background were so striking to me that I wanted to make a modern-day version of it with a laptop computer, green and yellow coloring, and my last name to replace the Adler title. With adobe illustrator, I tried to recreate the beautiful lines of Bernhard’s piece but wanted to use crisp and clean lines instead. For the colors, I chose green and yellow as I felt they would be an interesting contrast to Bernhard’s purple and orange. With this bookplate, my design was very much based on my own subjective ideas and concepts instead of following the original purpose of the Adler poster and advertising a product.
