Darwyn Cooke Tribute

A memorial for a legendary artist and writer

I designed a collage to pay tribute to Darwyn Cooke , the artist and creator of many groundbreaking graphic novel stories such as The New Frontier and Parker. I chose to incorporate his most popular pieces not only because they would be more recognizable to the general audience but also because they would help give the collage a theme. For this project, my main goal was to emphasize how much Cooke redefined DC comics and its many amazing characters, represented by all the characters in the collage coming from the self-portrait of Cooke in the bottom left corner.
