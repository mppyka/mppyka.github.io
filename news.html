Rethinking and recreating a space

For this class project, I digitally recreated a hallway on campus and added an exhibit to the final rendered work. After surveying several different buildings, I chose Haring Hall at UC Davis as it had a balance of complexity and simplicity in its door, stair, and wall designs. Once I finished the framework of the hall on Vectorworks and pasted the surface textures on Artlantis, I decided to rework the space as a tribute to Justin Ponsor, the late comic book artist who's colors and style inspired me to be an artist.
